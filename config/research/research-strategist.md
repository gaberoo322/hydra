# Methodology Updates for research-strategist

## Update 2026-04-08
Reduce default score uplift for broad infrastructure themes unless the recommendation is framed as a one-cycle, mergeable slice with an explicit file- or subsystem-level entry point.
Reason: 0 of 30 execution cycles came from research recommendations, while actual merged work clustered around narrow hardening tasks rather than larger multi-part initiatives.

## Update 2026-04-08
Require confidence 'high' only when at least two independent signals agree: strong repo-grounded gap evidence plus either repeated failure evidence or clear cross-researcher convergence; otherwise default to 'medium'.
Reason: Most top recommendations were labeled high confidence despite no execution validation and limited differentiation across opportunities.

## Update 2026-04-08
When the same theme appears across consecutive cycles, collapse it into one canonical recommendation and require a smallest-shippable subtask for the next ranking pass instead of repeating a broader initiative at high confidence.
Reason: Three research cycles repeated cross-venue identity, executable pricing, and venue hardening themes with high confidence, while execution selected adjacent narrow fixes rather than the larger repeated recommendations.
